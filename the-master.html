The Master - Ajay Jain

Acharya Dr. Ajay Jain

Ajay Jain with Maulan Ilyasi, Goswami Sushil Maharaj, Swami Chidanand Saraswati and Lokesh Muni.

Acharya Ajay Jain offering prayers at Maan Stambh Pran Pratishtha in Greater Noida.

Ajay Jain is a cheerful, interactive and humble person. Born on 15th August, he comes from a family of well-read and spiritually enhanced parents. After completing his higher studies, Ajay Jain transformed his life style as a socially, mentally and spiritually conscious person. Transformation begins with restlessness. If it converts into quest, it can lead to the awakening of the sub conscious mind, then to spiritual consciousness, social awareness and ultimately to enlightenment. The quest for self has lead him to the higher levels of consciousness which helps him helping the needy and other segments of the society works on Aura Level.

He believes that we all are part of the cosmos and there is more to the life than what meets the eye. There are unseen auras, unheard heartbeats and unfelt healing touches. Ajay Jain can rightly be called pioneer in Meditation and Vastu Remedies as he started Vastu consultations in the year 1986. He was the first one to offer Vastu Consultancy on National Channel during 1987 - 1988. “KNOW YOUR SELF TO KNOW OTHERS” is the thought that inspires him to work for all parts of the society. He is a Meditation Guru, a guide to many helping them learn Meditation techniques and practices. Since 1987, when he built a temple in Delhi using Vastu principles, he started using the same to rectify various types of Dosha that may happen in residential, commercial or any other type of premises to provide a trouble free and peaceful life to the occupants.

He is also a poet, a creative person and engages himself in regularly organizing cultural, social, religious and spiritual events for promotion of art, Indian culture and spiritualism. The urge to work for the welfare of the society came after self - realization. He is a social activist with a zeal for upliftment of all sections of society and ultimate aim of spiritual enlightenment of humanity at large. His doors are always open for the needy. Bad becomes good and good becomes better after meeting him. Since 1987, he is using Crystals for Energy Balancing, Healings and enhancing Positive Vibrations. For the last 30 years he is known as a social, cultural, spiritual and Vastu adviser to many. A popular figure amongst the residents, bureaucrats, industrialists, politicians, celebrities and film stars, Ajay Jain has helped many achieve their goals by Vastu remedies and is always available for everyone at anytime. He is associated to many Indian and foreign institutions and organizations and guiding them achieve their goals. Ajay Jain is an art curator working for promotion of art and artists of all forms.
