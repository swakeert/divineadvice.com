Life is simple but living can be tough, relations are simple but relationships can be bitter. It is said that one should have simple living and high thinking and keep pace with changing relations but confusion has to happen as actions and reactions can never be same as all are different personalities. Search of self and the divine is to complete the sphere of coming from the other. This other is the real test for all those who want to be at peace and feel the divine. All are born with certain possibilities. Possibility of becoming the ultimate in whatever we do. All have hidden powers and knowledge lies within but we lack awareness about our self and our karmas have no harmony with our thoughts.

Understanding the self, life and depth of relations and the divine within one has to keep a regular check on thoughts and actions. One tends to forget the potentialities he or she has resulting in a state of utter confusion and discomfort. Acharya Dr. Ajay Jain is the promoter of “Sambhav Sutra” that reminds you of Feasible You. Sambhav Sutra is a solution to all your physical, mental, social and spiritual quests and curiosities. Many a times one is stuck up in a situation, with a relation or with a question that needs immediate solution and one’s mind is not fully capable of providing an answer. This situation happens with everyone at some point.

Sambhav Sutra is a pleasant rescue for all those who want to seek proper guidance and want to have a clear idea of future thinking and actions. Sambhav Sutra is very popular among seekers and many send their queries that are answered regularly by Acharya Ajay Jain.
